Why Enigma exists

A hackathon designed like a protocol — every phase has a purpose, every team gets a fair shot at cracking it.

Mission

Give undergraduate builders a real 24-48hr constraint, a real problem, and a real shot at ₹25,000 — no gatekeeping, just teams and a clock.

Vision

Make SIES CSI's hackathon the one people plan their semester around — mentors who actually mentor, judging that's transparent, and outcomes people can put on a resume.

Why "Enigma"?

Because every real problem worth solving looks unsolvable first. Fintech and healthtech both hide their hardest constraints — we want teams who dig for them.

Two tracks. One code to crack.

Every team commits to a single track for the duration of the hackathon.

Track 01

FinTech

Build for the systems that move money — payments, fraud detection, financial inclusion, or the next layer of trust in digital transactions.

Track 02

HealthTech

Build for the systems that move care — diagnostics, accessibility, patient data, or tools that close the gap between symptom and solution.
